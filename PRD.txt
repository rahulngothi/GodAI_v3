1. Vision

Build the world’s most trusted AI-powered spiritual companion that enables users to interact with the wisdom of scriptures, saints, philosophers, and spiritual traditions through natural conversation, voice, and personalized guidance in their native language.

The platform will allow users to:

* Ask life questions rooted in spirituality and philosophy
* Understand scripture in simple language
* Receive daily guidance and reflection
* Interact with AI-powered spiritual personas
* Learn and explore faith traditions through conversation

Dharma AI aims to become the default spiritual operating system for the next billion users.

⸻

2. Problem Statement

Today spiritual knowledge is:

* Fragmented across books, videos, and commentaries
* Difficult to access in local languages
* Hard to contextualize for modern life
* Often dependent on finding the right teacher or institution

Users increasingly seek answers to:

* Purpose and meaning
* Relationships
* Career dilemmas
* Anxiety and suffering
* Parenting
* Death and grief
* Discipline and self-improvement

Current AI systems provide generic answers without grounding in authentic spiritual sources.

⸻

3. Product Vision

A user should be able to ask:

“I am unhappy in my job. What would the Gita say?”

and receive:

* Relevant verses
* Simple interpretation
* Multiple philosophical viewpoints
* Voice explanation in their preferred language
* Follow-up conversation

All responses must be traceable to original sources.

Feature 1: Ask Dharma

User asks any question.

Examples:

* What is karma?
* Why do bad things happen?
* Should I forgive someone?
* How do I control anger?

System provides:

* Answer
* Supporting scripture references
* Source citations
* Suggested follow-up questions

Feature 2: Multi-Perspective Wisdom

User can compare viewpoints.

Example:

Question:

Output:

Swami Vivekananda’s View

8. Trust & Safety

Critical Principle:

The platform must never fabricate scripture.

Every answer must include:

* Source text
* Verse reference
* Commentary source

The system must distinguish between:

* Scriptural truth
* Commentary
* AI interpretation

When uncertainty exists:

“Multiple traditions interpret this differently.”
